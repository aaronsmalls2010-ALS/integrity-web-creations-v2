Common Questions

Frequently Asked

How much does a website cost?

+

We build around your budget — always. After a brief conversation about your business and goals, we put together a transparent, itemized plan tailored to what you can afford. No surprises. No one turned away because of budget size.

Will I need to manage or update the site myself?

+

Not at all. Your monthly plan includes full management — updates, security, performance monitoring, and ongoing improvements. Your site is handled. You run your business.

How long does it take to build?

+

Most sites are live within 4–6 weeks from kickoff. Larger or more complex builds may take 6–10 weeks. You will always know exactly where things stand throughout the process.

Who will I actually be working with?

+

You work directly with A. Smalls — from the first conversation to launch and beyond. There is no hand-off, no junior account manager, and no disappearing acts. One person, fully committed to your project.

What do I need to have ready to get started?

+

Just a vision for your business and an idea of what you want your website to accomplish. We handle the strategy, design, content structure, and everything technical. You bring your business knowledge — we handle the rest.

Do you work with businesses outside of Beaufort?

+

Absolutely. While proudly rooted in Beaufort, SC, we serve businesses across South Carolina and nationwide. Everything is handled seamlessly with no drop in personal service quality.
